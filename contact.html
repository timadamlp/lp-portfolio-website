Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical process for working with a new client?

My process typically begins with an in-depth discovery session to understand your business, goals, and target audience. I then develop a tailored proposal with timeline and deliverables. After approval, I conduct necessary research, create initial drafts, refine based on your feedback, and deliver final content with any supporting recommendations.

How do you charge for your services?

I offer both project-based pricing and retainer options depending on your needs. Project-based pricing is ideal for one-time content needs, while retainers work well for ongoing content requirements. I provide transparent quotes based on project scope, complexity, and timeline.

How quickly can you deliver content?

Delivery timelines vary based on project scope and complexity. Smaller projects typically take 3-5 business days, while more complex projects may require 1-2 weeks. Rush options are available for urgent needs at an additional fee.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Yes! As a bilingual specialist (English and Mandarin Chinese), I work with clients globally. I'm comfortable with virtual collaboration and can adapt to different time zones for meetings and communication.
